Common Emergency Situations

Heart Attack

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea or cold sweat
  • Pain in arms or jaw

Immediate Action: Call emergency services immediately

Stroke

  • Face drooping
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech difficulty
  • Time to call emergency

Immediate Action: Remember FAST and call emergency

Severe Injury

  • Heavy bleeding
  • Broken bones
  • Head trauma
  • Deep wounds

Immediate Action: Apply pressure to bleeding, call emergency

Basic First Aid Instructions

Bleeding

  1. Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
  2. Elevate the injured area
  3. Apply bandage when bleeding slows
  4. Seek medical attention if severe

Burns

  1. Cool the burn under cold water
  2. Don't break blisters
  3. Cover with sterile dressing
  4. Seek medical help if severe

Choking

  1. Encourage coughing
  2. Give 5 back blows
  3. Perform abdominal thrusts
  4. Call emergency if not resolved
